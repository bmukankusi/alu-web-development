What is Beauty garden?

This is an idea, a project idea that is environmental conservation based. It will mainly work to sustain various plants species, specifically flowers and trees. This will serve as both a research and a touristic site.

Where will it be?

As from the picture from above, I wish this beautiful and magnificent garden will be in Rwanda.

Why this idea?

I always enjoy nature and animals.Trees, mountains, seas, aceans, rivers, animals, flowers, hills, and valleys all reflect beauty to me. Besides that vegetation is really important in this biodiversity and is worthy conserving. With this in mind, I wondered what I can do to contribute to the environment which is being polluted in these modern times, and there care an idea of the garden.How excited I am to see it come true!
